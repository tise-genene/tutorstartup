## üß≠ How We Operate

Eagle Tutorials Services acts as a trusted bridge between expert tutors and parents seeking quality education support for their children. Our process is designed to ensure fairness, accountability, and smooth collaboration.

### 1. Job Matching
We connect qualified tutors with parents based on subject expertise, location (for onsite), and availability.

### 2. Payment & Commission
Tutors pay **50% of the monthly amount as a one-time commission** for job placement.
- This payment confirms the tutor's commitment and secures the position.

### 3. Tracking & Relationship Management
We continuously monitor the tutor-parent relationship to ensure satisfaction on both sides and provide support when necessary.

## üí∏ Refund & Replacement Policy

We prioritize fairness and mutual respect. Our refund or replacement policy applies under the following conditions:

### 1. Parent-Initiated Termination
If a parent discontinues the tutoring service within the first month, Eagle Tutorials will:
- Offer a **full refund of the commission**, OR
- Provide a **replacement job with equivalent monthly pay**.

### 2. Tutor-Initiated Termination
If a tutor ends the job early:
- **No refund will be issued** unless there's a valid, verifiable reason beyond the tutor's control (e.g., health issues, family emergencies).

### 3. Mutual Agreement Termination
If both parties mutually decide to end the tutoring relationship, we assess the case individually and may:
- Issue a **partial refund**, OR
- Offer a **replacement opportunity**, depending on circumstances.

### 4. Performance or Misconduct Issues
If a tutor is removed due to misconduct, repeated lateness, or poor performance:
- The **commission will not be refunded**.
- Tutors are expected to maintain **professionalism, punctuality, and quality teaching standards**.

### 5. Replacement Guarantee Period
- Replacement offers are valid within **30 days of termination**.
- After that, refund or replacement eligibility may expire.
